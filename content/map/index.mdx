## Explore Campus Buildings Over Time

Use the slider to move through time and watch the Texas A&M campus take shape — from a handful of structures in 1876 to
the sprawling research university it became by the late twentieth century. Each marker represents a building in the
photographic collection.

Click any marker in the map below to browse images and learn more.
